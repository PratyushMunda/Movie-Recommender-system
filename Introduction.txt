//Recommender system:
it is a system which will help users to get recommendation using the history of the user.

//Types of Recommender system:

 <I>Content Based:
"When we recommend same type of content (On the basis of 'tags'). For example if a users listen to song of {Post Malone} the next song will be again of {Post Malone}"

 <II>Colaborative Filltering
"When we have two different users having similarity score of 9, if user1 watchs a movie M1 then this system will recommend M1 to user2"

 <III>Hybrid
"When we combine both Content based and Colaborative filltering system"
